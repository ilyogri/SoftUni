Breakthroughs in the Living Room

Whether we realize it or not, spectacular scientific breakthroughs can be found in the very heart of our homes. Let's take
TVs for example. Ask your grandparents whether they'd have thought color TV was ever possible in the age of black & white
screens. In retrospect, many may not have expressed such optimism.

Then there were the early 2000s when industry pioneers sold us the idea of being able to spot every blade of grass on the
football pitch as the advent of high definition TV dawned upon us. Industry naysayers expressed serious doubt as such a
transition would have required a major overhaul to equipment and broadcast standards.
